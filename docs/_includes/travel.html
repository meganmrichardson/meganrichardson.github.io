I was lucky to have the chance to travel around the world for half of 2024. This was my first experience solo-traveling and I loved the freedom and independence that came with having no set plan and everthing I needed in my suitcase. I visited 13 countries - all of which were brand new destinations for me!

One of my favorite memories from my time in Europe was going on midnight hikes in Norway. I visited the Lofoten Islands, way North of the Arctice Circle! It is the most magical place I have ever traveled too and would recommend visiting if you've never experienced Northen Scandanavia.

I became a Register Yoga Instructor in Ubud, Bali. The training was 200 hours of yoga flows, workshops, and lessons. Our lession were explorations of anatomy and history of yoga. I am so grateful to have met so many amazing friends through this transformative experience.
